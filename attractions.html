Here are some attracted places in Egypt

The pyramids

The 3 Pyramids of Giza are ancient tombs from the Old Kingdom of Egypt. The 3 pyramids were built by the following pharaohs: Khufu, Menkaure, and Khafre. They were built as symbols of power and burial places for the pharaohs.

Khan El Khalili

Khan El Khalili is hands down the most iconic bazaar in Egypt, right in the heart of Cairo's historic Islamic district. Steeped in history since the 14th century, this vibrant market is like a maze of alleys brimming with treasures, gold jewelry, leather goods, spices, and unique souvenirs.
